Meet Jim

Jim's work on workplace courage spans formal research, teaching, consulting, and writing for academic and practitioner audiences. As an academic with a distinguished track record that includes [Harvard, Darden, etc.] his work is always grounded in research. Jim's specialty is bringing concepts to life through innovative active-learning experiences that help individuals, teams, and whole institutions challenge limiting beliefs, change their behaviors, and own their agency.

Workplace courage is not about major acts; it's about many day-to-day opportunities.
